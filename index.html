About Flofentry Foods

Flofentry Foods is a trusted supplier of premium quality nuts in Kenya. We focus on delivering fresh, healthy, and carefully selected products to our customers across the country.


Our range includes almonds, cashew nuts, bitter kola and other nutritious snacks. All products are hygienically packaged to ensure quality and freshness.


We aim to make healthy snacking simple through an easy ordering process and reliable delivery service.
